The Problem

Climate change is the long-term shift in temperatures and weather patterns. These shifts should occur naturally, but since the 1800s, human actives have been the huge factor of climate change. Causes for climate change include burning fossil fuels (coal, oil, and gas), cutting down forests, carbon-dioxide emissions, and consuming too much goods like clothes, electronics, and plastics are all major factors of climate change. Everyday the situation worsen as we continue to omit large of carbon-dioxide, not recycling, and the using too much electricity. Ecosystems and agriculture is also in danger.

The Effect

Climate change has a detrimental effect on the environment as a whole. Some of the main effects of it are the extremely high temperatures that have been a result of climate change. Natural disasters such as storms, tornados, forest fires, hurricanes, heat waves, floods, droughts, and storms. Oceans are also starting to become more acidic due to industrialization which harms marine life majorly as well. The gasses emitted overall also can cause health problems for us. We need to make changes so we can continue to enjoy our favorite activities and visit new places before the effects of climate change get extreme.

Ways you can help

Everybody can do their part to help save our planet. There are several simple lifestyle changes that can make a big difference! Here are just a few ways we can do so:

  • Save energy at home: turn off lights, reduce the use of heaters and air conditioners, and more when it is not needed!
  • Walk, bike, carpool, and use public transport to limit carbon dioxide emissions
  • Avoid wasting food- use what you have before buying more!
  • Reuse, Reduce, Recycle
  • Eat less meat and dairy- save the animals and reduce carbon dioxide emissions.
  • Use reusable bags at the store and avoid the single use plastic
  • Shop eco friendly and sustainable brands for everyday essentials
  • Be mindful of what you buy and how much you buy for clothes, food, and more!
  • Conserve water- turn off taps, take shorter showers, and more!
  • Try using electric vehicles to reduce carbon dioxide emissions
By making these simple changes we are really able to make an impact! Use your voice so that we can save the planet and continue to enjoy the things we love.
